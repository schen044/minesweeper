Minesweeper

How To Play

Your goal is to try to deduce the locations of the mines. You win by revealing all of the safe tiles. Tiles that are suspected to be mines can be flagged. However, if a mine is clicked, then you blow up and lose.

Controls
  • Left click to reveal tile
  • Right click to flag tile
  • Click the smiley to reset the game
